Today's article is based on the unusual coronary disease named Angina


What is Angina

Angina is a type of chest pain caused by reduced blood flow to the heart. Angina is a symptom of coronary artery disease. Angina feels like squeezing, pressure, heaviness, tightness or pain in the chest. It can be sudden or recur over time. Angina is also known as Ischemic chest pain.

Symptoms include

Chest pain and discomfort
Dizziness
Fatigue
Nausea
Shortness of breath
and Sweating

Treatments for Angina

There is no specific cure for coronary heart disease or way to remove the atheroma that has built up in the arteries but treatments can help prevent the symptoms from getting worse.


Treatments includes;

  • The use of Blood-pressure lowering medications
  • The use of Salicylates to prevent excessive blood clotting e.g Aspirin
  • The use of Statins which are cholesterol lowering medicines
  • The use of calcium channel blockers
  • The use of nitroglycerins

Other healthy habits incudes;

  1. Eat a balanced healthy diet with limited amount of fat and cholesterol
  2. Avoid smoking
  3. Take a good rest when necessary
  4. Learn how to avoid stress
  5. excercise regularly

Contact your doctor if symptoms persist
